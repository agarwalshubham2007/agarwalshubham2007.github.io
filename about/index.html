About

I am currently a Research Software Engineer - AI at IBM Research, Cambridge, MA. My work has spanned from creating intelligent dialogue and conversation platforms for dialogue designers to using AI Planning in conversational/dialogue systems.

I graduated with a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Arizona State University in July 2017. During my master’s degree I worked in the field of Natural Language Understanding and Semantic Parsing of English natural language. My thesis is titled - “Aligning English Sentences with Abstract Meaning Representation Graphs using Inductive Logic Programming”.

I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani in the year 2015.
